Prevent Skin Disease follow below steps:
  1. Eat a healthy diet. A healthy diet can help you look and feel your best
  2. Limit bath time. Hot water and long showers or baths remove oils from your skin.
  3. Avoid strong soaps. Strong soaps and detergents can strip oil from your skin.
  4. Moisturize dry skin
  5. Block sun damage. Avoid intense sun exposure, use sunscreen, and wear protective clothing.
  6. Don't use tanning beds or sunlamps. They emit the same harmful UV radiation as the sun.
  7. In the morning, cleanse your skin to start the day then put on a moisturizing sunscreen. At night, cleanse your skin again to remove any residual makeup and sweat from the day then finish with a moisturizer.
